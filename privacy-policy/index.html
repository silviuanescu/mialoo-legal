Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MiaLoo ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Intended Audience

MiaLoo is designed for families, including parents and children. Certain features, including purchases and subscriptions, are accessible only through a parental gate intended for adult users.

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited information necessary to operate, improve, and measure the performance of our app.

  • Device and technical information (such as device type and operating system)
  • App usage information (such as interactions and app events)
  • Events such as app installs, app opens, and in-app purchases

We do not collect personal information such as name, email address, or precise location from children.

3. Use of Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers to help us understand how our app is used and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

These may include:

  • AppsFlyer (attribution and analytics)
  • Meta (Facebook and Instagram)
  • TikTok

These services may receive limited technical information and app event data (such as install and purchase events) to provide measurement and analytics services.

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and improve the app
  • Understand usage and engagement
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns
  • Support subscriptions and purchases

5. Parental Gate and Purchases

All purchases and subscription actions are protected by a parental gate. Children cannot access payment functionality without adult involvement.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited information with service providers strictly for analytics, attribution, and service improvement purposes.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

8. Children’s Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is designed to limit data collection and protect access to paid features through a parental gate.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, including access, correction, or deletion. You may contact us to exercise these rights.

10. International Transfers

We and our service providers may process information in countries outside your country of residence.

Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards under applicable data protection laws, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission or adequacy decisions.

You can learn more about these safeguards here:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Adequacy Decisions

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date.
