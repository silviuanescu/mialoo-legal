Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

How MiaLoo processes limited technical, support, purchase, and attribution-related information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Marian-Silviu Anescu (“we”, “us”, or “our”) processes information in connection with the MiaLoo mobile application (“MiaLoo” or the “App”), our support communications, and the related public pages linked from the App Store or Google Play listing.

MiaLoo is an educational app intended to be used by families. Some areas of the App may be explored free of charge. Access to paid content is protected by a parental gate before any billing page is shown.

1. Who controls the data

The data controller for MiaLoo is Marian-Silviu Anescu, Romania. You can contact us at assistance.mialoo@outlook.com.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information collected automatically

When MiaLoo is installed or used, we and our service providers may receive limited technical and event-based information needed to operate, measure, secure, and improve the App and its distribution. Depending on the device, operating system, platform rules, and service configuration, this may include:

  • app install, first open, app open, and similar app event timestamps;
  • basic app activity information, such as which app mode was opened or whether a paywall or locked item was viewed;
  • purchase-related events, including whether a monthly subscription or lifetime purchase was completed and the associated currency and value range necessary for revenue measurement;
  • device and app metadata such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, country or region, time zone, approximate network information, and platform-generated attribution data;
  • identifiers and attribution signals made available by the platform or SDK configuration, where permitted by the operating system and app settings.

We do not ask children to create an account in order to use MiaLoo.

2.2 Information you provide directly

If you contact support, we may receive the information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address, device details, screenshots, or the content of your request.

2.3 Payment information

All payments are processed by Apple or Google through the in-app purchase systems of the relevant app store. We do not receive or store your full payment card data.

3. Why we use information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • to provide and maintain the App;
  • to protect the App, prevent abuse, and keep the service functioning correctly;
  • to understand installs, app opens, subscription starts, purchases, and similar events;
  • to measure the performance of marketing campaigns and understand which advertising channels led to installs or purchases;
  • to analyze aggregate or event-based app usage trends and improve the user experience;
  • to respond to support requests and exercise legal rights;
  • to comply with legal, tax, accounting, or regulatory obligations.

4. Third-party service providers and SDKs

MiaLoo may use carefully selected third-party services to measure app installs, understand app usage, and evaluate whether advertising campaigns are effective. These services may process limited technical data and app events in accordance with their own terms and privacy notices.

We use these services to understand campaign performance and optimize distribution of MiaLoo. We do not require children to submit names, phone numbers, or email addresses to access the core app experience.

5. Parental gate and family safeguards

MiaLoo includes a parental gate before any billing page or purchase flow is shown. Locked content cannot be purchased directly from the child-facing interface. The parental gate uses an age-appropriate check designed to require adult participation before access to subscription or purchase screens.

For a dedicated public explanation, please also see our Parental Gate & Family Safeguards page.

6. Children and family use

MiaLoo is designed as a family educational experience. We seek to limit data collection to what is reasonably needed for app operation, security, support, purchases, attribution, and campaign measurement. We do not knowingly ask children to provide personal contact details in order to use the core educational content.

If you believe a child provided personal information to us directly through support or otherwise, contact us at assistance.mialoo@outlook.com and we will review the request.

7. Legal bases for users in the EEA, UK, and similar jurisdictions

Where applicable, we process information on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • performance of our contract with you, such as providing access to the App and premium content;
  • our legitimate interests, such as securing the App, understanding app performance, improving the service, and measuring campaign effectiveness in a proportionate way;
  • compliance with legal obligations;
  • your consent, where consent is required under applicable law or platform rules.

8. How long we keep information

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including support handling, transaction records, fraud prevention, measurement, legal compliance, and dispute resolution. Retention periods may differ depending on the type of data and the third-party provider involved.

9. International transfers

Our service providers may process information in countries outside your own. Where required, such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards under applicable law.

10. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, or withdrawal of consent where applicable. You may also have the right to complain to your local supervisory authority.

To exercise privacy rights, contact assistance.mialoo@outlook.com and include enough information for us to understand and verify your request.

11. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” signal. Because there is no uniform industry standard for responding to such signals across mobile apps and services, MiaLoo does not currently respond to browser-based Do Not Track signals in a specific way.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

13. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, your data, or MiaLoo generally, contact us at assistance.mialoo@outlook.com.
