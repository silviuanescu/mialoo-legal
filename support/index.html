Support

Last updated: March 21, 2026

How to contact MiaLoo support for billing, privacy, technical issues, and general assistance.

If you need help with MiaLoo, have a billing question, want to report a technical issue, or want to make a privacy-related request, contact us at assistance.mialoo@outlook.com.

1. What to include in your message

  • the name of the app: MiaLoo;
  • your device model and operating system version;
  • the store used (App Store or Google Play);
  • a short description of the issue;
  • screenshots, if helpful.

2. Billing and purchase help

MiaLoo offers a monthly subscription and a one-time lifetime purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple or Google. You can usually manage subscriptions, renewals, and cancellations from your App Store or Google Play account settings.

For general billing terms, see Subscriptions & Billing.

3. Privacy requests

If you want to ask about your data, request deletion of support correspondence, or submit another privacy-related request, email us with the subject line Privacy Request. More information is available in the Privacy Policy.

4. Parents and family safeguards

MiaLoo is designed as a family educational app. Access to billing or purchase pages is protected by a parental gate. More detail is available on the Parental Gate & Family Safeguards page.

5. Response times

We aim to review support requests as promptly as reasonably possible. Complex billing or privacy requests may take longer if we need additional information to verify the request or review store transaction details.
