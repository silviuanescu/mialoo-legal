Billing Summary

Subscriptions & Billing

Last updated: March 21, 2026

A plain-language summary of MiaLoo subscription, purchase, renewal, cancellation, and refund terms.

This page summarizes the main purchase and subscription terms for MiaLoo. It is intended to make store-facing disclosures easier to read. If there is any conflict between this page and the billing page shown by Apple or Google, the billing page and the applicable store rules control.

1. Available purchase options

  • Monthly subscription: recurring access to eligible premium MiaLoo content.
  • Lifetime purchase: one-time purchase intended to unlock eligible premium access, subject to store and platform limitations.

2. Pricing

Current prices are shown directly in the in-app billing page and may vary by country, store, taxes, exchange rates, promotions, or platform rules.

3. Renewal

Monthly subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period or as otherwise stated by the store. Renewal charges are processed by the relevant app store account.

4. Cancellation

You can cancel a subscription from your App Store or Google Play account settings. Cancellation stops future renewals but does not retroactively cancel charges already processed for the current period, unless the store grants a refund under its own policies.

5. Refunds

Refund requests are generally handled by Apple or Google as the merchant of record. We cannot guarantee refunds and cannot directly reverse store charges that were processed by the app store platform.

6. Access and restoration

If you purchased premium access and believe access was not restored correctly, contact support at assistance.mialoo@outlook.com with your device and store details.

7. Parental gate

Paid content is presented behind a parental gate before the billing page is shown. This is intended to reduce accidental child access to purchase flows.
